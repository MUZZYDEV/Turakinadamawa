Atiku Advocates for Crypto-Friendly Policies to Boost Youth Empowerment and Economic Growth

Atiku Abubakar has been a vocal supporter of cryptocurrency regulation, emphasizing that Nigeria should not stifle innovation but instead create a legal framework to allow the crypto sector to flourish.
